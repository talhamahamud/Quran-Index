About This Project

This website was born from a personal journey of reading and reflecting on the Qur’an.

While studying Tafheem-ul-Qur’an (tafsir by Abul A‘la Maududi), I often felt the need to see Qur’anic verses organized subject-wise across different surahs. Many themes—such as Jannat, about the prophets, guidance, warnings, and mercy etc.—appear throughout the Qur’an, but finding them together in one place can be difficult for readers.

From this need, the idea for this project came to my mind.

I began carefully listing Qur’anic ayat by subject, while also keeping them connected to their respective surahs, so that readers can navigate easily and reflect more deeply on each topic. This project is meant to help students, researchers, and general readers engage with the Qur’an in a more structured and meaningful way.

This is a continuous and evolving effort, and I am fully aware that mistakes or limitations may exist. If you notice any error, misclassification, or have suggestions for improvement, I sincerely encourage you to contact me through the provided contact information. Your feedback will help improve the accuracy and usefulness of this work.

My intention is simple: To serve the Qur’an, to make its study easier, and to help others reflect upon its guidance.

May Allah accept this effort and make it beneficial.
